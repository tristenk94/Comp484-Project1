Bolbi is a transfer student from Backhairistan. His appearance is of a large boy who wears a sweater with a green and orange vest.

He is usually comedic relief character in the Jimmy Neutron Series.

Bolbi is a very happy-go-lucky kind of guy. He is from a foreign land and his accent is very silly.

Despite his goofy demeanour, Bolbi is a very talented and well seasoned actor. His Shakespearean voice is one to behold. Check it out!
